About Smits & Wouters

Smits & Wouters is an independent commercial real estate consultancy founded in 1989 in Duiven, the Netherlands. We provide strategic guidance in real estate development, land positions, independent consultancy, and off-market transactions across Europe, Turkey and the UAE.

We are active across Europe with a particular focus on the Netherlands, Spain, Turkey, and the UAE (Dubai). Our off-market network gives us access to opportunities that never reach public listing platforms.

An off-market transaction is a private sale or acquisition that is not publicly listed or marketed. These deals are conducted discreetly between parties introduced through trusted networks. Smits & Wouters has 37 years of relationships that enable access to these exclusive opportunities.
