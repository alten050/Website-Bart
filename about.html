About the Firm

Independent Expertise Since 1989

Smits & Wouters is al sinds 1989 actief in de bemiddeling van binnen- en buitenlands vastgoed en heeft een reputatie opgebouwd binnen een netwerk van vermogende investeerders, family offices en beleggers.

From our office at Pastoriestraat 1 in Duiven, we advise clients across Europe, Asia (Turkey) and the UAE (Dubai). Our practice is built on discretion, long-term relationships, and a thorough understanding of complex cross-border real estate markets.

We specialise in development guidance, strategic land on A-locations, independent consultancy, and off-market transactions — serving institutional investors, family offices, and private capital with equal care and confidentiality.
